# What about sleep and wellness data?

Sleep, daily activity and wellness data is exported as local reports, not as a promise of Garmin Connect import.

1. Upload the ZIP from your Polar account and wait for wellness/sleep analysis.
2. Check counters for activity days, sleep nights, Nightly Recharge and daily heart rate.
3. Download Wellness ZIP if you want a local CSV, JSON or HTML archive.
4. Do not import these reports as sport activities in Garmin Connect.

Garmin Connect does not provide a stable public import for Polar Flow sleep history. Missing values remain empty or go to warnings; the app does not create fake zeros.
