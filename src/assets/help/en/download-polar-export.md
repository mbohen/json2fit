# How to download your Polar account export

Download the full data archive from your Polar account, not only from the Polar Flow app, and keep the original ZIP as a backup.

1. Open the Polar account export page: <https://account.polar.com/#export>.
2. Sign in with the same Polar account you use with Polar Flow.
3. Request the export and wait until Polar prepares the package.
4. Download the ZIP file to your computer. You do not need to unpack it before using json2fit.

The export can contain workouts, daily activity, sleep, account data and device metadata. Account/profile files are detected as sensitive data and are not included in sport export.
